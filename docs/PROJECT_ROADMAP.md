# SpaceX-Focused Self-Study Program Roadmap

**Goal:** ~1-year comprehensive self-study program to build a portfolio for SpaceX.

## Program Structure

### [Module 1: Foundations in Math, Physics, and Computation](src/Module_01_Foundations/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 8 Weeks
*   **Focus:** Calculus, Vectors, Differential Equations, Basic Python.
*   **Key Projects:**
    *   **Project 1: Rocket Ascent Simulator** (3D motion, drag, simple ML optimizer).
    *   **Project 2: Conic Orbit Visualizer** (Plotting orbital trajectories).

### [Module 2: Orbital Mechanics and Mission Design](src/Module_02_Orbital_Mechanics/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 8 Weeks
*   **Focus:** Orbits, Trajectories, GMAT/STK tools.
*   **Key Projects:**
    *   **Project 1: Starlink Orbit Propagator** (Propagating satellite positions).
    *   **Project 2: Starship Trajectory Planner** (Interplanetary transfer orbits).

### [Module 3: Space Systems & Propulsion Engineering](src/Module_03_Propulsion/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 8 Weeks
*   **Focus:** Rocket Engines, Systems Engineering, SMAD.
*   **Key Projects:**
    *   **Project 1: Starship Subsystem Trade Simulator** (Trade-off analysis: reusability vs payload).
    *   **Project 2: Constellation Designer** (Satellite constellation analysis tool).

### [Module 4: Human Factors, Regulations, and Space Policy](src/Module_04_Human_Factors/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 6 Weeks
*   **Focus:** Life Support, Space Law, Safety.
*   **Key Projects:**
    *   **Project 1: Crew Safety Simulator** (Modeling life support failure modes).
    *   **Project 2: Space Policy Risk Calculator** (Regulatory compliance tool).

### [Module 5: Astrophysics and Advanced Machine Learning](src/Module_05_Astrophysics_ML/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 8 Weeks
*   **Focus:** Space Data Analysis, Autonomy, ML.
*   **Key Projects:**
    *   **Project 1: Orbital Anomaly Detector** (ML model for anomaly detection).
    *   **Project 2: Solar Flare Predictor** (ML model using solar weather data).

### [Module 6: Electives and Capstone Integration](src/Module_06_Capstone/README.md)
*   **Duration:** 6+ Weeks
*   **Focus:** Final Portfolio Project.
*   **Elective Options:**
    *   Raptor Engine Propulsion Model
    *   Satellite Cybersecurity Simulator
*   **Capstone:** Final integrated portfolio showcase.
